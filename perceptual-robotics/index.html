Welcome!

Robotic platform Mingo has 3 degrees of freedoms controlled by 3 step motors.

It is equipped with 2 RGB cameras, 1 proximity sensor and 1 IMU.

Its controller is Raspberry Pi 4B, a strong single board computer which is capable of motor control, image processing and so on.

A Unix-like operating system Raspbian is pre-installed in the Raspberry Pi 4B.
